--- layout: page title: Pengchao Han subtitle: Postdoctor, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (Shenzhen) --- Pengchao Han is a Postdoctoral Researcher with School of Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (Shenzhen). She received the Ph.D. degree in communication and information systems in 2021 from Northeastern University, Shenyang, China. She conducted academic research at the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Imperial College London, United Kingdom, from Feb. 2018 to Aug. 2019. Her research interests include collaborative machine learning, mobile edge cloud networks, and wireless and optical technologies.

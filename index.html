--- layout: page title: Pengchao Han subtitle: Associate professor, Guangdong University of Technology --- Pengchao Han is an associate professor with the School of Information Engineering, Guangdong University of Technology. She received the Ph.D. degree in communication and information systems in 2021 from Northeastern University, Shenyang, China. She conducted academic research at the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Imperial College London, United Kingdom. She has been a Postdoc Research Associate with School of Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen, from 2021 to 2023.
